## Requirements

- macOS 13.0 or later
- Apple Silicon or Intel Mac

## Installation

1. Download the latest release from the releases page
2. Drag Uptime.app to your Applications folder
3. Launch the app - it will appear in your menubar
4. Right-click the menubar item to access preferences and history

## Usage

### Menubar Display
The app shows your current uptime directly in the menubar. The format automatically adapts based on the duration:
- Short uptimes: "45m"
- Medium uptimes: "3h 25m"  
- Long uptimes: "6d 22h 47m"

### Context Menu
Right-click the menubar item to access:
- **About Uptime**: App information and system details
- **Uptime History**: View all your uptime sessions and statistics
- **Preferences**: Customize display format and behavior
- **Quit Uptime**: Exit the application

### Preferences
- **Startup**: Enable launch at login
- **Display Format**: Choose time format style and visibility options
- **Update Frequency**: Balance between accuracy and battery usage
- **Notifications**: Toggle milestone notifications

## Privacy

Uptime is designed with privacy in mind:
- All data is stored locally on your Mac
- No internet connection required
- No telemetry or analytics
- No data is shared with third parties
